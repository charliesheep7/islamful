Sihan Cheng is the founder of Islamful, where he researches and curates Islamic content
for Muslim audiences worldwide. Drawing on training in applied analytics from McKinsey & Company Academy,
he approaches Islamic topics by aggregating established scholarly opinions and primary source evidence —
making traditional fiqh accessible without claiming scholarly authority.

His work focuses on presenting multiple scholarly perspectives accurately, citing the Quran, Sunnah, and
recognised classical scholars so readers can make informed decisions grounded in their own madhab and
trusted scholars.
